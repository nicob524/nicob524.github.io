The 'Koukles' Branch
As mentioned , Manolis Kouklakis had three
brothers, Georgios, Giannis and Petros. 
Giannis had four children: Manolis, Mihalis,
Nikolas and Garofalia.  
Manolis's children included a son Stelios,
who immigrated to America, where he married
Damascinio Gaitanis.  Their children were Mike
and George Koukles (deceased) of Tacoma, WA,
and Mary Koukles Zaccaria of Copperton, UT.
Mike's and George's descendants are living in
Washington state.
